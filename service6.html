Corporate Bank Account Opening in Dubai/ UAE

The UAE has one among the largest domestic banking sector in the Gulf region. Corporate Bank account opening in Dubai is not a complicated process. The formalities and documents required are almost the same for all banks.
Once you decide to open a corporate bank account in UAE, you can approach ECCRUMO CONSULTANTS, a renowned bank account opening consultant in Dubai, UAE.
We help you in opening business bank account in the below jurisdictions in UAE;
1) Mainland
2) Free zone
3) Offshore
4) Corporate Account
The requirements to open a corporate bank account in Dubai, UAE might be different for various banks.
Documents required for opening corporate bank account in Dubai: 1) Company Trade License
2) Certificate of Registration
3)Share Certificate
4) Company Memorandum & Articles of Association
5) Board Resolution empowering a Company Officer to open the account
6) Passport copies for shareholders and authorized signatories
